# Game Expansion Ideas for "It's Always Sunny in Space"

## Gameplay & Interaction

1. **Satellite Builder** — Let users design their own satellite by choosing components (solar panels, antennas, radiators, thrusters) that affect mass, power, and orbital capabilities
2. **Orbital Maneuvers** — Add thrust controls for Hohmann transfers, inclination changes, and station-keeping burns with a delta-v budget
3. **Mission Objectives** — Timed challenges like "photograph these 5 ground targets" or "rendezvous with another satellite"
4. **Debris Avoidance** — Spawn orbital debris and require collision avoidance maneuvers (Kessler syndrome scenario)
5. **Docking Mini-Game** — Approach and dock with a space station using RCS thrusters

## Simulation Depth

6. **Day/Night Ground Imaging** — Click on ground locations to "capture images" — only works in the right lighting/orbital geometry
7. **Communication Windows** — Ground stations that the satellite must pass over to downlink data, with realistic link budget constraints
8. **Power Management** — Solar panel orientation vs. orbit position affects available power; eclipse periods drain batteries
9. **Thermal Simulation** — Radiators must shed heat; sun exposure and eclipse transitions affect component temperatures

## Visual & World Expansion

10. **Multi-Satellite Constellation** — Deploy a constellation (Starlink-style) and visualize coverage patterns
11. **Other Celestial Bodies** — Add the Moon, show lunar orbits, or enable lunar transfer missions
12. **Space Weather Events** — Solar flares that degrade components or force safe mode
13. **Re-entry Visualization** — End-of-life de-orbit with atmospheric heating effects
14. **Aurora Effects** — Visible auroras at polar regions tied to solar activity

## Social & Progression

15. **Mission Log / Score** — Track completed objectives, orbital time, images captured
16. **Unlockable Orbits** — Start with LEO, unlock MEO, GEO, HEO, and Molniya orbits
17. **Agency Mode** — Manage a small space agency with a budget, launch schedule, and satellite fleet
